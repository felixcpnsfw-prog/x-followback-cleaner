## Style Prompt

Quiet technical walkthrough for an open-source Chrome extension. The video should feel like a calm product demo for a privacy-minded utility: dark UI, restrained motion, precise panels, readable labels, and a minimal cat creator mark. It should not feel like a hype ad or a neon crypto promo.

## Colors

- Background: `#07111f`
- Surface: `#111821`
- Soft surface: `#172231`
- Text: `#eef3f8`
- Muted text: `#9ba7b7`
- Primary accent: `#76a9fa`
- Success accent: `#5eead4`
- Caution accent: `#f59e0b`
- Line: `#2d3747`

## Typography

- Display: `Fraunces`, fallback `Georgia`, serif
- UI/body: `PingFang TC`, fallback `system-ui`, sans-serif
- Data/code: `IBM Plex Mono`, fallback `ui-monospace`, monospace

## Motion

- Medium-energy tutorial pacing.
- Use push-slide and soft crossfade transitions between scenes.
- Entrances should be precise: short y/x movement, opacity, small scale.
- Progress bars and scan overlays may have subtle shimmer, but no infinite repeats.

## What NOT to Do

- No loud sales language.
- No purple-blue gradient domination.
- No bokeh/orb-heavy background.
- No tiny UI text below video-readable size.
- No fake password/session harvesting visuals.
